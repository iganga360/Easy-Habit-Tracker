## ðŸ”§ Customization: Day-Specific Tracking

### What It Is
"Day-Specific Tracking" means assigning habits to ONLY certain days.

### How It Works
```
Habit: "Art of Pottery Class"
  â†“
Days Selected: Tuesday, Wednesday
  â†“
What Happens:
  - Checkboxes ONLY appear on Tue/Wed dates
  - Other days are blank (no clutter!)
  - Monthly goal auto-updates: 4 Tuesdays + 4 Wednesdays = Goal of 8
  - You only track it when you actually DO it
```

### Step-by-Step Setup
```
1. Open "Main Tracker" tab
2. Find the habit you want to customize
3. Click cell in "Days/Week Selected" column
4. Click the dropdown arrow
5. CHECK the days you want (M, T, W, etc.)
6. Leave UNCHECKED the days you don't want
7. Press DONE or click outside
8. âœ¨ Checkboxes instantly appear/disappear!
```

### Examples

**Structured Habit: Gym**
- Days: Monday, Wednesday, Friday
- Goal: 12 completions/month (3 per week Ã— 4 weeks)
- Only checkboxes on M, W, F dates
- Can't accidentally check Saturday

**Class Habit: Yoga**
- Days: Tuesday, Thursday
- Goal: 8 completions/month (2 per week Ã— 4 weeks)
- Only Tuesday & Thursday checkboxes shown
- Easy to see your attendance

**Appointment Habit: Therapy**
- Days: Wednesday (once per week)
- Goal: 4 completions/month
- Only Wednesday checkboxes available
- Perfect for recurring appointments

Track flexible habits without assigned days. You get ALL dates available.

### How It Works
```

  â†“

  â†“
What Happens:
```

## âš ï¸ Troubleshooting

### Checkboxes Not Appearing
**Problem**: Selected days but no checkboxes showing
**Solution**: 
1. Click the habit row
2. Verify the date columns are visible (not hidden)
3. Unhide columns if needed (right-click â†’ Unhide)

### Goals Not Updating
**Problem**: Changed days but goal stayed the same
**Solution**:
1. Click the "Goal" cell for that habit
2. Press Enter to recalculate
3. Or manually set your goal number

### Progress Not Calculating
**Problem**: Daily/weekly/monthly progress not updating
**Solution**:
1. Click a checkbox to verify it works
2. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
3. Check if formulas are protected
4. Contact support if issue persists

### Can't Edit a Cell
**Problem**: Cell is locked/protected
**Solution**:
1. Some cells are protected to prevent accidental deletion
2. Unprotect: Tools â†’ Protect sheets and ranges
3. Uncheck the protection for that range
4. Or contact support for help
