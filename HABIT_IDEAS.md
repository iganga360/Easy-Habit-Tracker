## ğŸ¯ How to Use These Templates

### Step 1: Pick Your Template
Choose the one that aligns with your PRIMARY goal right now.

### Step 2: Customize
- Swap habits as needed for your life
- Adjust times to fit your schedule
- Add personal twists

### Step 3: Add to Tracker
- Type habit names exactly as listed
- Set realistic goals for each
- Assign days if applicable

### Step 4: Commit
- Track for 30 days
- Don't change habits mid-month
- Celebrate progress!

### Step 5: Evolve
- After 30 days, assess what worked
- Keep your wins
- Add new habits
- Remove what didn't serve you
